# Normal law

## Definition

:::definition
Let $m \in \mathbb{R}$ et $\sigma > 0$. $X$ follows the Normal law with the parameter $m$ and $\sigma^2$ if its density is :

$$
f_X(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^2}}exp\left(-\frac{(x-m)^2}{2\sigma^2}\right).
$$

We note $X \sim \mathcal{N}(m, \sigma^2)$.
:::
