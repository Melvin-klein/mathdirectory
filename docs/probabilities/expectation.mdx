# Expectation

## Definition

:::definition
Let $X$ be a positive real random variable, then its expectation is defined by the quantity, may be infinite,

$$
    \mathbb{E}[X] := \int_\Omega X(\omega)d\mathbb{P}(\omega) = \int_\mathbb{R}xd\mathbb{P}_X(x).
$$
:::
